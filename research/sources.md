## Manual LinkedIn collection steps

LinkedIn was inaccessible for many individual post URLs because public search did not index the post pages or LinkedIn public fetch returned cache-miss/login-limited pages.

1. Log into LinkedIn in a browser.
2. Visit the expert's profile URL from the sections above.
3. Open the Activity or Posts tab.
4. Filter recent 2025-2026 posts where possible.
5. For each useful post, save the post URL, visible date, full text or screenshot, and optional engagement counts into the matching file under `/research/linkedin-posts/`.
6. Prefer posts on LinkedIn organic strategy, founder/operator narrative, pricing/PLG/GTM frameworks, audience research, category positioning, product launches, or zero-click/in-feed content.
